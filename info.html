FAQ

What defines a "Musa Bhai" Inspired scent?
Our "Inspired" collections are high-fidelity captures of world-renowned luxury fragrances. We use 1:1 ingredient mappings to ensure the scent profile and longevity match or exceed designer standards without the exorbitant marking.
What is the performance profile of your fragrances?
Our Signature Perfumes are formulated for 10-12 hours of skin performance. Our Concentrated Oils (Attars) provide a dense projection and can remain detectable for 24-48 hours on textiles.
Are these products authenticated?
Every bottle is verified for composition purity. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for synthetic fillers, ensuring a 100% skin-safe and natural-inspired scent journey.
